## Current Role

This preserved package is a supporting Galaxian history / cabinet-spec /
image-lead artifact, not primary timing truth.

It is most useful for:

- release, developer, and licensing metadata for `Galaxian`, including Namco
  authorship and Midway licensing/distribution notes
- cabinet and hardware facts such as vertical raster monitor, mono sound,
  `2-way` joystick plus `1` fire button, and cabinet-style variants
- concise gameplay summary prose and a short game-introduction description
  that can be cross-checked quickly against other preserved sources
- preserved image-lead references for marquee, cabinet, bezel, control panel,
  side art, PCB, title, additional screenshots, flyers, and manual listings
- trivia and documentary side notes, including the RGB-color claim and the
  `Galaxip` naming clarification

## What It Does Not Replace

This source does not replace:

- arcade-era manuals
- measured gameplay video
- extracted motion windows
- direct audio or timing evidence
- promoted score-policy or stage-five-and-beyond gameplay windows

Its main value is cabinet/spec grounding, short documentary prose, and visual
lead discovery, not authoritative frame or cue timing.

## Current Role

This preserved package is a supporting Galaga player-guide and screen-surface
still bundle, not primary timing truth.

It is most useful for:

- explicit prose about attack-phase strategy, including when to score enemies in
  flight versus in formation
- player-language for boss capture timing, dual-fighter use, and challenge-stage
  value
- score-grounding prose for common enemies, boss-in-flight variants, and bonus
  enemies
- survival advice around side-clearing, safe-space creation, and staying alive
  long enough to capitalize on scoring opportunities
- supporting still-image leads for the Galaga logo, title screen, tractor beam,
  and early gameplay HUD/formation surfaces

## What It Does Not Replace

This source does not replace:

- arcade-era manuals
- measured gameplay video
- extracted motion windows
- direct audio or timing evidence
- challenge-stage subclips and explosion lifecycle still bundles

Its main value is player-strategy interpretation plus a useful screen-surface
still bundle, not authoritative frame or cue timing.

# Galaga (1981) Namco Manual

Source artifact:
- `Galaga_-_1981_-_Namco.pdf`

Why it matters:
- documents official gameplay rules, stage/challenge structure, scoring, capture behavior, and presentation flow
- includes cabinet/manual imagery that can help with visual fidelity decisions

Initial observations captured from the gameplay section:
- Stage 1 enemies do not bomb during fly-in; later stages do.
- The first challenging stage appears after Stage 2, then every third normal stage after that.
- Challenging stages consist of 40 enemies in 5 groups of 8 and award additional bonuses for complete 8-enemy groups plus a 10,000-point perfect bonus.
- Special three-enemy attack squadrons appear from Stage 4 onward and award escalating bonuses when completely destroyed.
- After game over, the original game shows a results/statistics screen before high-score initials entry.
- A captured fighter stays with the specific command ship that captured it; shooting the captured fighter itself awards points but destroys it.

Local derived analysis output for this manual is intentionally left out of version control.

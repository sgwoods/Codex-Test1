## Current Role

This preserved package is a supporting Galaga history / cabinet-spec / image-lead
artifact, not primary timing truth.

It is most useful for:

- release, developer, and publisher metadata, including Namco and Bally Midway
  release framing
- cabinet and hardware facts such as vertical raster monitor, mono sound,
  2-way joystick plus one fire button, and cabinet-style variants
- concise gameplay summary prose that is short enough to cross-check against
  other preserved sources without rereading a long strategy guide
- preserved image-lead references for cabinet, marquee, bezel, control panel,
  PCB, title, and additional gameplay screenshots
- cheat, trick, and bug documentation that corroborates tractor-beam edge
  cases, anti-fire setup, score-rollover notes, and the demo-control bug

## What It Does Not Replace

This source does not replace:

- arcade-era manuals
- measured gameplay video
- extracted motion windows
- direct audio or timing evidence
- challenge-stage subclips and explosion lifecycle still bundles

Its main value is cabinet/spec grounding, short gameplay summary prose, and
visual lead discovery, not authoritative frame or cue timing.

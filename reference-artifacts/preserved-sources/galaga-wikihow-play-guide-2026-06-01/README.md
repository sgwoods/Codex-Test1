## Current Role

This preserved package is a supporting Galaga player-guide and annotated
screen-surface bundle, not primary timing truth.

It is most useful for:

- explicit prose and annotated stills for the anti-fire setup and the
  "game is set" waiting rule
- capture and rescue guidance, including not shooting the captured fighter and
  waiting for the boss on its live run instead of destroying it in formation
- entrance-pattern explanation, including double-side, doubled-up, and
  single-file language
- opening-stage positioning guidance, including the slightly-right-of-center
  opening attack and "bug out" timing description
- score and threat policy examples, including red-versus-blue priority, morph
  bonus explanation, later-stage wrap-speed warning, and challenge-result bonus
  stills
- a stronger annotated-still lane for Aurora policy explanation than prose-only
  guides provide

## What It Does Not Replace

This source does not replace:

- arcade-era manuals
- measured gameplay video
- extracted motion windows
- direct audio or timing evidence
- challenge-stage subclips and explosion lifecycle still bundles

Its main value is annotated policy explanation and supporting still evidence,
not authoritative frame or cue timing.

## Current Role

This preserved package is a supporting Space Invaders history / cabinet-spec /
image-lead artifact, not primary timing truth.

It is most useful for:

- release, developer, and regional distribution metadata for `Space Invaders`,
  including `Taito` authorship and `Midway` North American release context
- cabinet and hardware facts such as vertical standard-resolution raster
  display, `black and white with a color overlay`, amplified `mono` sound,
  `2-way` controls, and `1` fire button
- control-model clarification that the Bally/Midway North American version used
  left/right button movement rather than a conventional joystick feel
- concise gameplay summary prose that can be cross-checked quickly against
  other preserved rules and history sources
- preserved image-lead references for marquee, cabinet, bezel, control panel,
  side art, PCB, screenshots, flyers, and manuals

## What It Does Not Replace

This source does not replace:

- original manuals or score-table page captures
- measured gameplay video
- extracted timing windows
- direct gameplay audio context
- bunker, cannon, or invader-row close-reference still bundles

Its main value is cabinet/spec grounding, short documentary prose, and visual
lead discovery, not authoritative frame or cue timing.

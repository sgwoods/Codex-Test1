## Current Role

This preserved page is a supporting Space Invaders history / strategy /
player-review artifact, not primary timing truth.

It is most useful for:

- explicit player-language about dynamic tension and why the speeding march
  motif matters so much to the live feel
- prose grounding for the one-shot projectile constraint and how that changes
  late-wave pressure
- descriptive strategy around column-chopping and holding the horde off the
  screen edge
- control-model interpretation, especially the original left-button /
  right-button cabinet feel versus later joystick assumptions
- historical framing for why Space Invaders became the cultural and mechanical
  jump point that Windigo should respect

## What It Does Not Replace

This page does not replace:

- measured gameplay video
- extracted timing windows
- direct gameplay audio or march-tempo evidence
- original manuals, score-table stills, or cabinet presentation sources

Its main value is player-experience interpretation and historical framing, not
authoritative measurement.

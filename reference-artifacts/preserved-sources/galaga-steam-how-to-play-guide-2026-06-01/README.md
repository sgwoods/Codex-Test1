## Current Role

This preserved package is a supporting Galaga player-guide and still-image
artifact, not primary timing truth.

It is most useful for:

- explicit prose about dual-fighter capture and rescue flow
- player-language about dodging practice and not getting cornered at the screen
  edges
- challenge-stage positioning and shot-timing advice, especially around patience
  and not spamming shots
- modern player-guide framing for Select Round practice workflows
- one small readable still-image lead for Galaga-family alien-body color and
  shape reference

## What It Does Not Replace

This source does not replace:

- arcade-era manuals
- measured gameplay video
- extracted motion windows
- direct audio or timing evidence
- challenge-stage subclips and explosion lifecycle still bundles

Its main value is player-strategy interpretation and a small supporting still,
not authoritative frame or cue timing.
